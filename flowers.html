Amaryllis Flower

The stunning amaryllis belladonna is known as Naked
Lady in the US, The smooth-textured foliage grows
in early summer then dies back. In August or
September the flowers stalks come up without
foliage, producing 2 to 12 frogrant, funnel-shaped,
as well as cut flowers.

Anemone Flower


The Anemone genus is part of the Ranunculaceae (buttercup) family. There are a little over 120 species of anemones in a wide range of colors. Anemones are popular in gardens as individual species flower in the spring, summer, or fall, providing continual color.

Jasmine Flower


Jasmine has been known the world over for its sweet.
exotic fragrance for thousands of years. With more than
200 different species of jasmine worldwide, it's not
surprising that there are some special variations on the
common jasmine variety often seen in gardens.
